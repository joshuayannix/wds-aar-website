The All-American Rejects

ABOUT

The All-American Rejects are a rock/alternative genre band. They were one of my favorite bands growing up. Some of their best songs include Dirty Little Secret, Move Along, It Ends Tonight, Swing Swing, Stab My Back, Night Drive, Top of the World, Paper Heart.

Move Along was definitely their best album, full of absolute bangers. I don't really like any of the stuff they came out with afterwards. The most "recent" song I've listened from them was "Gives You Hell," and while it's not a bad song, I remember I hated it because it was completley different from Move Along. Other similar bands I listened to and liked during this time are Green Day, Three Days Grace, Red Jumpsuit Apparatus, Breaking Benjamin, and Boys Like Girls. Don't judge I bet you like these bands too.
